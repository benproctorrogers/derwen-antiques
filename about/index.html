About Derwen Antiques

Content block 1

Derwen Antiques have provided quality antique fairs in and around Carmarthenshire for over 20 years.

We showcase a wonderful variety of quality antique, vintage and retro collectables to suit all tastes and budgets.

Our fairs provide a friendly and vibrant atmosphere and we regularly welcome the crew and contestants from BBC One’s Bargain Hunt.

Follow us on Facebook for updates on our fairs.

About our antique fair locations

National Botanic Garden of Wales

Llanarthne, Carmarthenshire, SA32 8HG

Come and enjoy the spectacle of Welsh art, Welsh pottery and Welsh oak furniture amongst the Mediterranean planting in Sir Norman Foster’s Glasshouse.

Our flagship event is held in the National Botanic Garden of Wales. The Gardens are spread over 560 acres of beautiful Carmarthenshire countryside, with an amazing collection of 8,000 plant varieties. They are also home to the world’s largest single-spanned glasshouse, with a world-famous display of Mediterranean plants.

The event has grown in size and popularity, with over 60 stands in the Glasshouse and more dotted around interesting areas of the Gardens, including the Beehive Theatre, the Large Marquee and the impressive Regency Principality House.

The Botanic Garden welcomes dogs during the Antique Fair weekends.

Llandeilo Civic Hall

Crescent Road, Llandeilo, Carmarthenshire SA19 6HN

Our vibrant antiques and vintage fair has been established for many years in the heart of the bustling town of Llandeilo at Llandeilo Civic Hall. Held on the first Saturday of every month, the fairs have a mix of antiques including jewellery, furniture, textiles, vintage clothes, books, silver, pocket watches and an amazing collection of pewter.

The Civic Hall hosts many community groups and events and is within walking distance of a public car park. Llandeilo sits at the western edge of the Brecon Beacons National Park. Find out more about visiting Llandeilo.
